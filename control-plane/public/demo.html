AI agents are powerful enough to actually do things. That's exactly what makes them risky.

An agent that can send an email, move money, or read a customer record can also leak it, overspend, or do something you never intended — and most of them run with full access to whatever they're handed.

So projects stall. Security and legal can't sign off on something they can't see or contain. Diffract is built to remove that blocker: real capability, with guardrails your team can actually approve.

What Diffract gives you

Capability you can trust.

01

Your data stays yours

Agents only reach the systems you approve — and never see your passwords or keys. Even in a worst case, there's simply nothing for them to leak.

02

You stay in control

Decide exactly what each agent can do and touch. Tighten it, widen it, or switch it off in seconds — the boundaries are yours to set, not the AI's.

03

Everything is on the record

Every action an agent takes is logged in plain sight. Audit, compliance, and your own peace of mind are covered from day one.

04

Nothing is locked in

Use any AI model, your existing tools, and your own accounts. Switch whenever you like. You're never tied to a single vendor's roadmap.
